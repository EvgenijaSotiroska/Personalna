Cooking

Cooking is a passion I inherited from my grandparents. Ever since I was 4-5 years old, my grandma used to put me on the table so I can help her make some dough for pizza or meatballs for my soup. I always feel very fulfilled when I cook. Because I am staying at undergraduate dormitories that doesn't happen very often, that's why when I am back in my hometown I try to use the most of the time to cook for my family and friends.

Reading books

Me loving books, is a habit that came from my mother. She LOVES books, and has read over 1000 books in her life. She always knows what kind of book to recomend to me and I always enjoy them. She also has written two books, so you get the idea how important books are to her. One intresting fact about me is that, I either don't read books for months, or I read four in one week - and there is no in between. Most of the time I read thrillers, actions or mystery, but sometimes when I want to relax, I read romance or comedies.
